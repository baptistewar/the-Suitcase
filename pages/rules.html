RULES

To play the game "in my suitcase", the first player has to complete the phrase "in my suitcase, there is ..." with a word, expression or other.
Exemple : the player makes it "in my suitcase, I have a car."

The next player has to retype all the words (or other) in the same order and then add a new one himself

The game continues until one of the player fails to retype the whole sequence before adding his own new item to it.
